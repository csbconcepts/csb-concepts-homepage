Origin Story

From Brand Owner to Agency Founder

  • Started by building and scaling his own supplement and consumer brands on Amazon — learning the platform from the inside as an operator, not an observer.
  • Experienced firsthand the limitations of manual campaign management and the broken promises of traditional agencies that didn't understand unit economics.
  • Built proprietary AI systems to manage his own brands more effectively — automating bid optimization, inventory forecasting, and competitive analysis at a level no human team could match.
  • Realized the AI infrastructure he'd built could serve other brands at scale, delivering the same operator-level performance across an entire portfolio.
  • Founded CSB Concepts to bring operator-level AI to the broader Amazon ecosystem — combining the instincts of a brand owner with the precision of purpose-built artificial intelligence.
