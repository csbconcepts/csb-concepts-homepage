1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to the site owner. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until they expire or you delete them) or "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, local storage, and similar tracking mechanisms. References to "cookies" in this policy include these similar technologies where applicable.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. The methods for doing so vary by browser. Here are instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data
  • Safari on iOS: Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
  • Chrome on Android: Settings > Site settings > Cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some websites. Since our Site does not currently rely on first-party cookies, disabling cookies should not impact your experience on our Site.

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising through industry tools such as:

  • The Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out page at optout.networkadvertising.org
  • The Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) opt-out page at optout.aboutads.info

Since we do not currently employ tracking technologies on our Site, no additional action is required on our part to honor DNT requests. Should we implement tracking technologies in the future, we will update this policy to describe how we respond to DNT signals.

As stated above, CSB Concepts does not currently use cookies to collect personal information, does not sell personal information, and does not engage in targeted advertising on this Site. For more information about your privacy rights, please see our Privacy Policy.
