From $38K/mo to $100K/mo in 9 Months

A consumer goods brand with solid product-market fit but zero Amazon strategy. No storefront, no A+, running broad match only.

Scaling a consumer brand on Amazon requires more than just increasing ad spend. Many brands hit a revenue ceiling because their PPC campaigns are poorly structured, their listings lack conversion-optimized content, and they have no brand presence through storefronts or A+ pages. This consumer brand had strong products and loyal customers but was leaving significant revenue on the table due to broad match keyword targeting, lack of brand storytelling, and no DSP retargeting strategy. CSB Concepts applied its full-funnel AI-powered Amazon growth framework to systematically eliminate waste, build brand equity, and unlock scalable revenue channels that tripled monthly sales within nine months.

Consumer brands that plateau on Amazon almost always share the same root causes: inefficient PPC structures bleeding budget on irrelevant terms, missing brand assets that fail to convert browsers into buyers, and no retargeting strategy to recapture lost shoppers. CSB Concepts addresses each of these issues systematically using AI-powered bid management that adjusts thousands of keywords daily, professional brand content creation that builds trust and drives conversions, and Amazon DSP retargeting that brings back high-intent shoppers. The result is not just more revenue but better margins, because every dollar of ad spend works harder. For consumer goods brands looking to scale past their current plateau, the combination of AI technology and experienced Amazon operators is the difference between incremental growth and transformational results.
