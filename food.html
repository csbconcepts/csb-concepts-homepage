◆ THE CHALLENGE

Why Food Brands Need AI on Amazon

Food is the only Amazon category where your product literally expires on the shelf. Add Subscribe & Save complexity, FBA temperature restrictions, and a buyer base obsessed with dietary labels — and you need more than a generic agency. You need AI built for food.

⏰

Expiration Date Management

Amazon requires minimum shelf life for FBA inbound — typically 90+ days remaining. If your product approaches expiration, Amazon can suppress your listing or charge removal fees. Food brands lose an average of 8-12% of inventory to expiration-related waste on Amazon. That's pure margin destruction.

✓ Our AI tracks expiration dates across every unit in FBA, forecasts sell-through velocity, and automatically triggers promotional campaigns (coupons, Lightning Deals, PPC boosts) when inventory approaches the expiration threshold — converting potential waste into revenue.
🔄

Subscribe & Save Optimization

Subscribe & Save is the single most important growth lever for food brands on Amazon — it creates predictable recurring revenue and increases CLV by 3-5x. But most agencies set it and forget it. The wrong discount tier, frequency, or enrollment funnel leaves massive recurring revenue on the table.

✓ Our AI continuously optimizes S&S discount tiers, enrollment incentives, and frequency recommendations based on your product's actual consumption patterns. We've increased S&S enrollment rates by 30-50% for food brands through data-driven tier optimization.
🍵

Freshness Perception & Reviews

Nothing kills a food listing faster than reviews mentioning "stale," "expired taste," or "old product." Even one negative freshness review can tank conversion rates for weeks. Food buyers are hyper-sensitive to freshness cues in images, copy, and reviews — more than any other category.

✓ Our AI monitors reviews for freshness-related sentiment in real-time, triggering alerts when negative freshness mentions appear. We build listing content that proactively communicates freshness — packaging dates, batch freshness guarantees, and storage instructions that build buyer confidence.
🌱

Dietary Preference Keyword Targeting

Food search on Amazon is driven by dietary preferences — "keto snacks," "vegan protein bars," "gluten-free crackers," "organic baby food." These long-tail dietary keywords have high purchase intent but require deep category knowledge to target effectively. A generic agency doesn't know the difference between "paleo" and "whole30" shoppers.

✓ Our AI builds keyword architectures around dietary preference clusters, matching your product's certifications and ingredient profile to the exact search terms your target buyer uses. We target 200+ dietary modifier keywords per food brand.
