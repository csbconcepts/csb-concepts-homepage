◆ THE CHALLENGE

Why Supplement Brands Need AI on Amazon

Supplements are one of the most competitive and heavily regulated categories on Amazon. Generic agencies don't understand the compliance minefields, restricted keywords, or the review dynamics that make or break supplement brands.

⚠

Compliance & Restricted Keywords

Amazon's supplement advertising policies are a minefield. One wrong claim in your listing or ad copy — "cures," "treats," "prevents" — and your ASIN gets suppressed. Most agencies learn this the expensive way. Supplement brands lose an average of $12K per listing suppression event.

✓ Our AI scans every listing and ad for 2,400+ restricted terms in real-time, flagging violations before Amazon does. Zero compliance-related suppressions across our portfolio in the last 12 months.
★

Review Velocity & Social Proof

Supplement shoppers are skeptical. They read reviews obsessively — 94% won't buy a supplement with fewer than 50 reviews. But Amazon's review policies make it harder than ever to build that social proof organically, especially for new launches.

✓ Our AI-powered Vine optimization and follow-up sequencing accelerates review velocity by 3.2x. We strategically time enrollment windows and optimize insert card compliance to maximize review generation within Amazon's TOS.
📈

Brutal PPC Competition

Supplement CPCs have increased 34% year-over-year. Category keywords like "vitamin D" or "probiotics" cost $4-8 per click. Without AI-driven bid management, your ad spend bleeds into unprofitable clicks while competitors with deeper pockets dominate page one.

✓ Our AI adjusts bids every 15 minutes across 50,000+ keywords per account, shifting budget to high-converting long-tail terms that generalist agencies ignore. Average CPC reduction of 28% in the first 60 days.
📦

Complex Variant & Bundle Strategy

Supplements live and die by their variant strategy — count sizes, flavors, bundle packs, subscribe-and-save tiers. The wrong parent-child structure tanks your BSR. The wrong hero ASIN cannibalizes your own listings. Most agencies don't understand supplement-specific catalog architecture.

✓ Our AI analyzes purchase patterns across your catalog to identify the optimal variant hierarchy, hero ASIN, and bundle configurations that maximize both conversion rate and average order value.

Compliant Listing Optimization

Supplement listings require a delicate balance: compelling enough to convert, compliant enough to avoid suppression. Our AI generates and tests copy that maximizes keyword indexing while respecting Amazon's restricted claims policies for dietary supplements.

A+ Content & Brand Story

Premium A+ content designed for supplement buyers who need trust signals — clinical study callouts, ingredient sourcing transparency, manufacturing certifications. We build brand stories that reduce return rates and increase Subscribe & Save enrollment by up to 40%.
