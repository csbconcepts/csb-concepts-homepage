## Submission Workflow

1. **Prepare assets first:** Logo (square + horizontal), 150-word bio, 300-word description, 3 case study summaries with metrics, team headshots
2. **Submit High Priority first** — these have the biggest SEO and LLM training impact
3. **Keep NAP identical** across every listing (name, address, phone, website URL with consistent trailing slash)
4. **Collect reviews** — Clutch, G2, GoodFirms, and Google Business Profile reviews are the primary ranking factor within these directories
5. **Track in Status column** — use: Submitted / Live / Pending Review / Needs Reviews / N/A
6. **Re-check quarterly** — update descriptions, add new case studies, respond to reviews
