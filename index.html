Lost inventory, damaged goods, overcharged fees, missing inbound shipments — Amazon makes mistakes every single day. Most sellers never notice. We audit every transaction in your account and file claims to recover what's rightfully yours.

Our reimbursement team combines deep Seller Central expertise with AI-powered auditing to catch discrepancies most agencies miss. No upfront cost — we only get paid when you get paid.
